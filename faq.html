Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about admissions, courses, scholarships and coaching programs at Verchas Academy.

What courses does Verchas Academy offer?

Verchas Academy offers NEET, IIT-JEE, CUET, B.Sc Nursing, Foundation (Class 11–12), Pre-Foundation (Class 9–10) and Board Tuition programs.

Where is Verchas Academy located?

Verchas Academy is located in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh.

Does Verchas Academy provide NEET coaching?

Yes. Verchas Academy provides comprehensive NEET preparation including classroom teaching, tests, doubt sessions and mentorship.

Does Verchas Academy provide IIT-JEE coaching?

Yes. Verchas Academy offers IIT-JEE Main and Advanced preparation programs.

Are scholarship tests conducted?

Yes. Verchas Academy regularly conducts scholarship tests and merit-based fee waivers for eligible students.

Are Foundation classes available?

Yes. Foundation and Pre-Foundation programs are available for Classes 9, 10, 11 and 12.
