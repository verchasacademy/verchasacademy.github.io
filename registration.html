1. Filling up of this form does not guarantee admission. Students must visit the Academy Office for final registration.
2. Registration at the Academy Office begins from 15 March 2026.
3. Students are not required to submit fees at the time of office registration. They may avail up to 15 days of demo classes to assess the academy, faculty, and curriculum before submitting fees as per academy instructions.
4. Documents required for registration at the Academy Office:
   (i) Passport size photographs ‚Äì Qty: 02
   (ii) Copy of Aadhar Card
   (iii) Copy of 10th or 12th Marksheet (or both, whichever is applicable)
   Original documents must be produced for verification.
5. In case any wrong, misleading, or false information or documents are submitted, the admission may be cancelled at any time.
